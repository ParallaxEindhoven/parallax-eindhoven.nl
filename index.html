Information

What is the dress code?

We encourage business casual. While formal attire isn't required, we appreciate a style that complements the 170m perspective.

Are walk-ins welcome?

Always. However, due to our limited capacity at this altitude, we highly recommend securing a reservation to guarantee your view.

Private Event Pricing?

Exclusive floor hire begins at €400 per hour (excl. VAT), including two of our premier mixologists. For bespoke arrangements or multi-day bookings, contact events@parallax.nl.

Private Event Policy?

We host a variety of curated gatherings. To respect our District-E neighbors, we specialize in sophisticated atmospheres rather than high-decibel events.
